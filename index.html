Reality or Fairytales?

When reality and fairytales collide, is it much fantastic and wonderful as we hoped it would be? Real-Life fairytales is a story about real life tangled with unbelievable tales from our wildest and grandest imagination.

When characters from childhood books come to life, and hopes and wishes do come true, are you ready for it? A song and scene cycle written by Ejay Yatco, directed by Missy Maramara, brought to life by choreography of Franco Ramos;

Real-Life Fairytales consists of different tales that wipes the fine line between blandness of reality and grandeur of fantasy.

Real Stories from Real People.
